Some of the essential features are :

    Powerful Search

    Built on the WhizzKid KnowledgeBase, our powerful search and matching algorithm enables detailed Subject search and accurate Teacher matching.

    WhizzKid automatically identifies and books the right Teacher for you. Choose to receive instant help or schedule a date and time in the future. Like your Teacher? Book regular sessions.

      Live interaction between Student and Teacher in a video and whiteboarding Session with document sharing. After your lesson, view a recording of your entire Session and read all the notes made.
